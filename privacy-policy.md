# Privacy Policy for TokCreator

Last Updated: December 2025

---

## Introduction

TokCreator ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application TokCreator (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Content Input**: When you use AI features (Caption Generator, Hashtag Generator, etc.), you may provide topics, keywords, or other text input. This information is sent to third-party AI services to generate content for you.
- **Language Preference**: We store your language preference (English or Indonesian) locally on your device using SharedPreferences.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: We may collect information about your device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
- **Usage Data**: We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as which features you use and when you use them.
- **Advertising Data**: Google AdMob may collect information for advertising purposes (see Section 4).

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- We do NOT require user registration or login
- We do NOT collect location data
- We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
- We do NOT track your activity outside the App

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 To Provide Services
- **AI Content Generation**: Your input (topics, keywords) is sent to third-party AI services (Groq API) to generate captions, hashtags, ideas, and scripts.
- **App Functionality**: We use your language preference to provide the App in your preferred language.

### 2.2 To Improve Our Services
- We may analyze usage patterns to improve App performance and user experience.
- We use error logs and crash reports to fix bugs and improve stability.

### 2.3 For Advertising
- We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may use your device information to show relevant ads (see Section 4).

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

### 3.1 AI Services
- **Groq API**: We use Groq API to power our AI features. When you use AI features, your input is sent to Groq's servers. Please review [Groq's Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy) for information about how they handle your data.
- **RapidAPI**: We may use RapidAPI services for TikTok-related data. Please review [RapidAPI's Privacy Policy](https://rapidapi.com/privacy) for information about how they handle your data.

### 3.2 Advertising Services
- **Google AdMob**: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information about your device and usage to show relevant ads. Please review [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [AdMob's Privacy Policy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543) for more information.

### 3.3 Analytics Services
- We may use analytics services to understand how users interact with the App. These services may collect anonymous usage data.

## 4. Google AdMob

### 4.1 AdMob Data Collection
Google AdMob may collect the following information:
- Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID)
- Device information (model, OS version)
- App usage data
- Location data (if permitted)
- Other information as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 4.2 AdMob Data Usage
- AdMob uses collected information to:
  - Show relevant advertisements
  - Measure ad performance
  - Prevent fraud
  - Comply with legal requirements

### 4.3 Opt-Out of Personalized Ads
You can opt-out of personalized ads by:
1. Going to your device Settings
2. Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Note: You will still see ads, but they may be less relevant to you.

## 5. Data Storage

### 5.1 Local Storage
- **Language Preference**: Stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data is not transmitted to our servers.
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do NOT store your content or preferences on our servers.

### 5.2 Third-Party Storage
- AI service providers (Groq) may temporarily store your input to process requests. Please refer to their privacy policies for data retention policies.

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted to third-party services is encrypted using HTTPS.
- **Local Storage**: Data stored locally on your device is protected by Android's security features.
- **No Personal Data**: We do not collect or store personally identifiable information.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
